Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2, 2026

Spoken English Class ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when using our website and practice tools.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to operate this website:

  • Contact form details — such as your name, email address, and message, when you voluntarily submit the contact form.
  • Practice input — the English translations you type during practice exercises. This is processed locally in your browser to give you feedback and is not stored on our servers.
  • Usage data — general, non-identifying information such as browser type and pages visited, used to improve site performance.

How We Use Your Information

Any information collected is used only to:

  • Respond to your inquiries submitted via the contact form.
  • Improve the quality, accuracy, and usability of our practice exercises.
  • Monitor and maintain the security and performance of our website.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Cookies and Local Storage

Our website may use minimal cookies or browser local storage to remember your preferences and improve your experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time without affecting your ability to use the practice tools.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect any information submitted through our contact form. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Our platform is designed to be suitable for learners of all ages, including students. We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information from children without appropriate parental guidance.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data you have submitted to us (such as contact form entries) by emailing us at the address below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page along with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@spokenenglishclass.com.
