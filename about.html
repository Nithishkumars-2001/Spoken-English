About Us

Helping Tamil Speakers Learn English, One Sentence at a Time

Our mission is simple: make spoken English practice accessible, clear, and encouraging for everyone.

Our Story

Why We Built This Platform

Many Tamil speakers understand English grammar rules but struggle to translate their thoughts confidently and correctly in real time. Traditional classrooms often lack instant, personalized feedback.

Spoken English Class was created to close that gap — offering a focused space where learners can translate real Tamil sentences into English and immediately see what they got right, what needs improvement, and why.

Whether you're a student preparing for interviews, a professional improving workplace communication, or simply someone who wants to speak English more confidently, this platform grows with you.
